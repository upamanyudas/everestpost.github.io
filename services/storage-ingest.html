In a data-intensive production environment, robust media management is critical to project success. {{ site.title }} offers comprehensive storage and ingest solutions that combine cutting-edge technology with streamlined workflows to ensure your content is always secure, accessible, and efficiently managed.

Our services span the entire media lifecycle—from initial ingest and active project storage to long-term archival preservation—providing you with scalable, reliable infrastructure that adapts to your project's specific needs and budget.

Our Storage & Ingest Services

High-Performance Storage

Our enterprise-grade storage infrastructure combines SSD RAID arrays with intelligent tiering to deliver exceptional performance for active projects while optimizing costs for less frequently accessed media.

Archival Solutions

Secure your valuable content with our comprehensive archival services, featuring LTO-8 tape libraries and polar storage options for long-term preservation with reliable retrieval systems and detailed metadata tracking.

Media Processing

Our GPU-accelerated render farm handles large-scale media processing with precision and speed, supporting ACES color management, automated QC, and delivery to editorial via secure networks or Aspera file transfer.

Workflow Design

Every project has unique requirements. Our team collaborates with you during pre-production to design custom workflows and technology solutions that maximize efficiency while respecting your budget constraints.
